EleVee is a sustainability project development from Alam Sutera Development.  PT Alam Sutera Realty Tbk (Alam Sutera) is a leading integrated property developer in Indonesia since 1994 engaged in the construction and management of housing, commercial areas, management of shopping centers, recreation centers, and hospitals (integrated area development). Property development by Alam Sutera prioritizes innovation to improve the quality of human life.
